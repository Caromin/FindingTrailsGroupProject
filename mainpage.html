Hello, Welcome to our 1st group project. Below is an explanation of our website.

  1. Enter any location in the search bar to the left.
  2. Once a location is entered, up to 10 markers will display on the map, within a 50mile radius, this is based on TrailAPI parameters.
  3. (On Hiatus) Filters can be used based on activities that each marker provides, please note that TrailAPI has determined 90%+ of the locations as mountain biking & hiking.
  4. We have also provided a current location and temperature section after you have inputted a location in the bottom left corner.
  5. If interested please take a look at our About Us page to learn more about the team that created this.
